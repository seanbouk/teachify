# Henry VIII's Wives

The six wives of King Henry VIII of England, in order. The classic
mnemonic for their fates is "divorced, beheaded, died, divorced,
beheaded, survived."

```q ... ``` block. The first line is the question, the second is the
answer. Anything outside those blocks is ignored — feel free to edit
