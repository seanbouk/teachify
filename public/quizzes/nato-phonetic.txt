# NATO Phonetic Alphabet

The 26 callsigns used by aviation, military, and emergency services
to spell letters unambiguously over radio.

Each question is in a fenced ```q ... ``` block. Anything outside
those blocks (including this paragraph) is ignored by the app, so
feel free to add notes or trivia anywhere. The optional `#id:` lines
keep your progress when you edit a typo.

```q
What is the NATO callsign for the letter A?
Alfa
#id: a
#alt: Alpha
```
